After I get my Bachelor's degree, I plan to get a Master's degree in something creative. I'm using this last year of getting my Bachelor's degree to try and discover which path to take. I am originally from Alaska, but now I live in New York City. My dream is to buy a big beautiful cabin, big enough for everyone to visit, in Midway, Utah where my Anderson ancestors are from. That would also be a nice location for my film-producing husband. Sundance is nearby and hopping a plane to LA or NYC from the SLC airport would be a snap.
