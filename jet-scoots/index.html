Happiness is ~~> Renting wheels from ScOOts!

Imagine yourself riding on one of our fun and affordable ~ScOOts scooters with the sea breeze blowing through your hair. As you take in stunning vistas of the turquoise ocean, pristine sandy beaches, lush foilage, and exotic wildlife, your favorite person is seated right behind you, holding on tight!

Come to Cozumel Island, off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea! ~ScOOts has everything you need to get around. Two convenient locations: Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry Dock and the Terminal Puerta Maya. Rent a Honda Metro, Honda Dio, or Honda PCX scooter. For a bigger group, rent a Honda Pioneer, or a 4-door or 2-door Jeep Wrangler. Check out Rentals to get the scoop on ~ScOOts. Then go to Reservations ~ reserve yours and get ready for fun!
