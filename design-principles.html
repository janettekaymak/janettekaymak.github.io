Alignment

amazon.com

Alignment on Amazon.com is especially important because there are so many images and content. In this example, you can see both horizontal, as well as vertical alignments. Both text and images are in alignment.

White Space

facebook.com

Facebook makes an excellent use of white space. Facebook is endless and goes on forever, so it's very important that it isn't too cluttered. I like the way the images and colors are alternated with white space and text.
