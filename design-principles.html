Janette Kaymak

Alignment

churchofjesuschrist.org

I love the way The Church of Jesus Christ website has aligned their website. The images are lined up both assymetrically and symetrically. The alignment creates a very pleasant view for the eyes and creates a peaceful feeling, in my opinion.

White Space

facebook.com

Facebook makes an excellent use of white space. Facebook is endless and goes on forever, so it's very important that it isn't too cluttered. I like the way the images and colors are alternated with white space and text.
